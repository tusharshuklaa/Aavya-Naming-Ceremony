MEANING OF "AAVYA"

The name "Aavya" defines as "The first rays of sun". The way the first rays of sun fills our days with the abundance of energey and power, the same way this little angel has filled not only our life but the lives of our entire Dubey and Shukla family.

Also, the name "Aavya" has been derived in a way that it takes first half from my name i.e. "Aashish" whilst second half from "Shai"vya. Hope our little angel would like it when she would grow enough to understand the meaning of her name.
